GotYou Parental Control App

The survey was conducted on 75 parents and it concluded that children 4-15 years of age, spend about 2-4 hours everyday on YouTube. 38.7% parents complained about meltdowns once TV is shut down.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

James, the caring and concerned father of two girls, is annoyed by constant pop-up of inappropriate videos on YouTube that his daughters often watch unknowingly. The increasing visual stimulation is causing behavioural changes and constant meltdowns in his young kids, disrupting the family environment.

We came up with a few ideas for our persona and asked our peers to vote for the best ones. Based on votes, a priority board was created with ideas that had high impact andhigh value.

This is the storyboard of James who is busy at work while his kids are enjoying TV time at home. James and his wife are concerned about what kids watch on TV and are noticing behavioral changes in them. James finds GotYou app, downloads it, and sets it up. He is now able to monitor YouTube videos, and track TV time. His family is now happy and much calmer than before.

Before designing the wireframes, we looked at competitors and their strengths.

PRIORITY MATRIX

User flow was iterated several times based on feedback and suggestions. A dashboard was adding with filters, screen time, delete options.


FEW MID TO HIGH FIDELITY SCREENS



The GotYou app helps parents add the YouTube profile of kids and monitor it on their phone by tracking screen time, filter content by setting preferences, and also delete profile. It will alert parents if any inappropriate video is played by tracking keywords.


FINAL THOUGHTS AND NEXT STEPS

Being in the early stages, a lot of user feedback is needed to ensure quality service to customers. It is a much needed parental control app that is very simple to use and straight forward. Going ahead, we have in mind the following features:

  • Two-Factor Authentication for adding YouTube profile
  • Add other platforms used for watching videos, like Amazon Prime, Disney, Netflix etc
  • Advanced settings>Enter modem details to block inappropriate videos at the root
  • Set Screen time and turn off screen
  • Improve service design by taking constant feedback from users
